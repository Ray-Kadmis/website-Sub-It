Responsibilities

The responsibilities of every competent handyperson at a company can vary. However, we ensure our clients have a handyman who can adapt and deliver efficient results depending on the organisation's specific needs. They are very good at understanding the nature of their industry and, therefore, provide reliable and quality solutions to every problem.

Communication

As a handyman, you will be asked to communicate with various departments. It is essential because an organisation can only function with proper, effective communication. You will have to talk with supervisors, colleagues, and other stakeholders.

Discuss their maintenance issues, repair status, and other potential impacts on the organisation in detail with everyone around you. Ensure you provide regular updates and reporting on the completed tasks. Communicating with other departments is also one of your responsibilities to ensure timely resolution.

Documentation

Keeping a track record is also one of your core responsibilities as a handyman. You must have accurate records of the maintenance activities that are being performed in the organization. Every repair performed and installed equipment must be noted in that document. It will help the organization in keeping track of various maintenance schedules. You will also be able to identify recurring issues and ensure compliance with every regulatory requirement.

Inventory management

Managing the inventory is also one of your responsibilities. It means you will be asked to keep track of supplies, tools, and other equipment required for repair work. You must also ensure all the maintenance spare parts are available in your inventory. Ensure the inventory is regularly updated with spare parts, tools, and materials.

Emergency response

Emergency response is also one of your responsibilities. You must be available to respond In an emergency. You might be asked to work on maintenance issues outside regular business hours. So be ready to tackle problems like power outages, plumbing emergencies, or other structural damage requiring immediate attention.

Safety compliance

Safety compliance is one of the primary responsibilities of every handyperson. You must be able to ensure that the company facilities are safe. All equipment at the company or construction site must comply with safety standards. Make sure that everything is regulated according to the guidelines of that area. It also involves identifying potential hazards and, therefore, implementing safety measures.

Installation

Installing new equipment can be arduous, so you must be proficient and skilled enough to install equipment and fixtures. You must also be able to perform installations on various furnishings if needed. Assembling furniture, mounting shelves and installing lighting fixtures can also be asked of you. You may also be required to set up various office equipment.

Repair work

The repair work is vital. It will constitute more than 80% of your job as a handyman. You must be able to address various types of repairs promptly. You must efficiently handle these requests. So be ready and be prepared to fix broken equipment. You will also be asked to repair damaged structures and replace faulty components. It also involves troubleshooting issues to identify the root cause of problems.

Routine maintenance

Routine maintenance is an important task that the handyman must conduct. You will be asked to complete regular inspections and maintenance tasks to ensure the company facilities function appropriately. It also involves ensuring that the equipment and every system in the organisation is in good working condition. You will be asked to check for plumbing leaks and other electrical fixtures. These electrical fixtures may even require repairing and replacing. You will be asked to service HVAC Systems and perform general upkeep tasks.

Requirements

We are a recruitment agency that deeply cares about the satisfaction of our clients, which is why we ensure that every candidate we choose for the handyman role is skilled and qualified. We make sure that every candidate passes through rigorous screening tests to ensure that he is competent. There are requirements we ask from our candidates, which are mentioned below.

  • We always prefer candidates who are experienced. However, even if you are not experienced, we will be ready to provide you with opportunities. We seek experience in general maintenance, repair, and other handyman work. All experience as a Carpenter, plumber, or electrical worker will be considered.
  • We ask our candidates for certifications and licenses if they have any. It provides insight into their hard work and talent as a handyman. However, if you are experienced, we do not require these certifications and licenses as long as you are committed to providing efficient results.
  • We value characteristics such as reliability and dependability in a candidate. Here, we ensure that you have the necessary qualities to be reliable if we choose you as a candidate. These qualities include being punctual and owning up to your mistakes. You must be able to meet the deadlines in a prompt and timely fashion.
  • We make sure that we assess the candidate's communication skills in both verbal and written form. It is because communication is highly effective in understanding the needs of our clients. It is also essential to provide updates on the work progress and even coordinate with other team members.
  • In every candidate we hire, we ensure he has the necessary problem-solving abilities as a handyman. You must be able to troubleshoot issues independently rather than depending on others. You must be able to diagnose, analyse, and resolve maintenance problems independently.
  • We ensure our candidates are physically fit because this job requires you to be strong. You need to have the stamina to be able to work long hours. Besides that, we also ensure that our candidates can safely lift, bend, climb, and operate heavy equipment Or tools.
